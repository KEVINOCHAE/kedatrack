About Us

Kedatrack Limited is an IT Services Provider Company whose solutions range from IoT solutions such as Advanced FLeet Management; automobile GPS tracking, fuel level monitoring and Theft detection and Cold Storage/ Cold chain Temperature & Humidity Monitoring solutions, Ai Powered Video Telematics (Dashcams, DSM , ADAS cameras) to Security services such as CCTV Cameras and Access Control systems. Further, we also provide Secure Innovative Cutting-Edge software Solutions; Web Design & Mobile App Development. Our solutions are backed by state-of-the-art technology in smart sensing, telecommunications, and cloud computing.

Additionally, we also offer General merchandise Supplies services; providing a wide range of products and services to private and government sectors, including computers and other IT hardwares and accessories, office furniture and equipment, printing supplies, staff uniforms, etc. The company aims to develop long-term partnerships with customers by consistently meeting and exceeding their expectations.
