About Us

Kedatrack Limited is an ICT Services Provider company whose solutions range from Full-stack Software Development, IT Systems (APIs) Integration, Automobile GPS Tracking, Fuel Level & Drain-off Monitoring, Fleet Management, Temperature Monitoring (cold storage/refrigerated trucks), Video Telematics, CCTVs Installation, ICT Consultation Services, etc. Our solutions are backed by state-of-the-art technology in Smart Sensing, Telecommunications & Cloud Computing & Software Engineering Solutions. Additionally, we provide a wide range of Products Supplies to private and government sectors, including computers and other IT/IoT & Networking Hardwares and accessories. The company aims to develop long-term partnerships with customers by consistently meeting and exceeding their expectations.
